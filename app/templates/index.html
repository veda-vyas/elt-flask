English Literacy Test


Welcome to the Home Page of the English Language Comprehension Test. The purpose of this test is to evaluate your English Language Comprehension. The English Language Comprehension test consists of 4 components:

I. Listening Comprehension Test
II. Speaking Test
III. Reading Comprehension Test
IV. Writing Test

Listening comprehension test is for 15 minutes and evaluates your English listening skills.

Speaking test is for 5 minutes and evaluates your capability of listening skills in English.

Reading Comprehension test is for 20 minutes and evaluates your capability of reading and comprehending English language.

Writing is for 20 minutes and evaluates your capability of writing in English which includes vocabulary, grammar, structure and flow.

All the questions of this test carry 1 mark. Writing question carries 7 marks and speaking section carries 6 marks.

Total Marks: 25
Total Time: 60 Minutes
